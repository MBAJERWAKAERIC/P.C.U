Embrace Generosity

One of our core values is “We Embrace the Mission” and we can do that by being a generous church. Our mission to glorify God by making disciples is possible because of your generosity.

God has designed us to be generous in ways that expand His kingdom here in Western North Carolina and around the world. We get to be conduits through which His generosity flows. We give because He gave first and our giving shows that we trust God.

Giving is to be a priority, done proportionally, sacrificially, and cheerfully. (Proverbs 3:9; Malachi 3:8-12; Matthew 6:19-21, 25:14-29; Luke 21:1-4; Acts 2:44-47, 20:35; 1 Corinthians 4:1-2; 2 Corinthians 9:6-15)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online giving safe?

Yes. We take very seriously the security of your personal information and data. Your payment information is encrypted and cannot be seen by our staff.

Can I give through my bank account?

Yes. Choose the Bank Account option when selecting your payment method. You can give using a checking or savings account.

Can I manage my account online or view my giving history?

Yes. You can adjust your giving or view your giving history at any time by clicking the button below!

View My Account

How do I get a contribution statement?

Annual contribution statements will be sent by mail or email after the end of the calendar year. You will receive your statement no later than January 31.

What is the deadline for annual contributions?

All contributions must be received or postmarked by December 31 to be included in your contribution statement for that year. Any online contributions must also be initiated and dated as of December 31.

Can I make a non-cash gift (stock, property, etc.)?

Yes. Please contact our Contributions Assistant by email at contributions@biltmorechurch.com or call 828.687.1111 and we would be glad to help.

Does Biltmore Church undergo an annual audit?

Yes. Batts Morrison Wales & Lee, P.A. conducts a yearly audit in accordance with auditing standards generally accepted in the United States.
